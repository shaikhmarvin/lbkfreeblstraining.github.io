Lubbock Free CPR/BLS Training

Free, community-based lifesaving education

Our Mission

Lubbock Free BLS Training is a medical student–led, volunteer community initiative dedicated to providing free, AHA-aligned Basic Life Support (BLS) training to youth, laypersons, and community members. Our mission is to improve emergency preparedness while expanding workforce readiness through accessible, high-quality lifesaving education.

What We Offer

  • Free CPR & Basic Life Support training
  • Youth and community-focused instruction
  • Training delivered through authorized AHA Training Centers
  • Early exposure to healthcare and emergency response careers

Who We Serve

Our programs are open to youth, students, laypersons, and community members interested in learning lifesaving skills or exploring healthcare and emergency response careers.

Get Involved

We welcome volunteers, instructors, and community partners. If you are interested in supporting or participating in our programs, please reach out.

Partners & Sponsors

We are actively seeking partnerships with hospitals, foundations, community organizations, and local businesses that share our commitment to improving emergency preparedness and workforce readiness in West Texas.
